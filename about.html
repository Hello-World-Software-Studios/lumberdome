About Lumberdome

Lumberdome is about survival. Given a ticking clock of starvation, an axe, and the meagre resources in the wilderness around you, survive as many days as you can! Sticks can be used to craft hunting gear, a cot, kindling, and more. Logs can be used to craft shelters like a lean-to, or campfire. Manage your resources wisely, and you can stave of starvation long enough to be rescued!

Welcome to Lumberdome!

It is just you and your axe. Use it to harvest trees for sticks and logs. Use the wood to survive as many days as you can!
